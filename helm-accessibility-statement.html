Accessibility Statement for Access to Words Helm Wayfinding Application

This accessibility statement applies to the Helm wayfinding application.

This application is designed to help users navigate large or complex venues and aims to be accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

We are committed to making this application accessible in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standard.

What to do if you have difficulty accessing content

We are always working to improve the accessibility of our application. If you have difficulty accessing any content or using any features, please contact us:

https://accesstowords.com/

Please provide us with as much detail as possible about the issue you are experiencing, including the specific page or feature you are trying to access, the type of assistive technology you are using (if any), and any error messages you receive. We will do our best to respond to your request within 5 business days.

Reporting accessibility problems with this application

If you find any accessibility issues that are not listed in this statement, please let us know. We welcome your feedback and will take it into consideration as we continue to improve the accessibility of our application.

What we are doing to improve accessibility

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of our application. Our ongoing efforts include:

  • Addressing the known accessibility issues listed in this statement.
  • Conducting regular accessibility audits.
  • Providing accessibility training to our development team.
  • Incorporating accessibility considerations into our design and development processes.
